Golazo: social reconciliation strategy through sports

Golazo seeks to strengthen the social network and prevent violence through the promotion of soccer in boys, girls, teenagers and youth, bringing together sports components, the use of leisure time with an educational sense, training and family coexistence. Likewise, it promotes the construction of peace and reconciliation, the training in citizen values, coexistence and peaceful conflict resolution through sports and education.

Golazo prevents the participants from being recruited by illegal groups, the use of psychoactive substances, school drop-outs, family and community violence.

    Direct beneficiaries
  • 3.000 boys, girls, teenagers and youth.
  • 940 families.
  • 28 community sports monitors.
