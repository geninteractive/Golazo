Golazo: Social reconciliation strategy through sports

Is a boys and girls soccer programdesigned to prevent youth violence and strengthen social networksin the communities it serves. Bringing together sports, and the use of leisure time with educational activities,youth benefit greatly from this important program. At the same time Golazoalso promotes the construction of peace and reconciliation, family coexistence the training in citizenship values, as well as coexistence and diplomatic conflict resolution through sports and education.

    Direct beneficiaries of this program include:
  • 3,000 boys and girls, both teenagers and youth.
  • 940 families.
  • 28 community sports monitors.
