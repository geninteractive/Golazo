Saberes Early Education: A fantasy with imagination and affection

Saberesis a program focused on the training of educational agents (preschool teachers or “community mothers”) who in-turn contribute to comprehensive and high quality care of boys and girls in their early years.To date Saberes has trained and supported over 1,713 educational agents who serve 18,712 boys and girls per year in vulnerable areas in Colombia.

The Carvajal Foundation supports ongoing training programs by providing a space for training that cultivates professionalization and family values while supportingeach agent for 300 hours. The Foundation also supplieseducational materials helping them become more empowered early childhood educators.




Through this project, the Carvajal Foundation Works inconjunction with the public policies of the national government and the Sustainable Development Objectives of the United Nations, contributing to the improvement of the quality of early education in Colombia.
